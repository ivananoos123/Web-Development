EDUCATION



Elementary

I graduated Elementary at the age of 13 at year 2014 in Don Vicente Rama Memorial Elementary School. As I remembered Iam the salutatorian of my batch(Public Sections) back then. Actually, there's two salutatorian, one from the Special Science Class Curiculum and one from the Public Sections.

Junior High School

Highschool life is one of the best life I've ever had. It is so memorable that I wont ever forget every single moments. During highschool, I'm just your typical highschooler boy. But I studied very well that since grade 7 until grade 10 I'm a consistent honor student. I lived highschool to the fullest. I experience a lot of things like cutting classes, experienced infatuation, and many other crazy things. Don Vicente Rama Memorial National Highschool is one of the best school I've ever had.

Senior High School

After the moving up ceremeny in Junior Highschool, I decided to explore new environment. So, I enrolled Senior High School in Cebu Institute of Technology - University as a STEM student and I never regret my decision until now. Senior High School is the best 2 year high school I've experienced in my life. I never expect that strangers will turn into special, close friends. For a short span of time, I've experienced a lot of things that made me happy. I'm also blessed that I'm a consistent honor student since Grade 11 until Grade 12. I will never forget my senior high school life.
